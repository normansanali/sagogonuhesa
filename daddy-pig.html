Daddy Pig (real name Percy Pig) is an animated character of the British preschool animated television series. He is also commonly known as Mr. Pig in the TV show titled “Peppa Pig”.

Percy is the father of the main titular protagonist of the series of the same name. Furthermore, the program first made a debut on May 31, 2004, and aired on Channel 5 and Nick Jr.

The director and producer of the hit series are Astley Baker Davies. Moreover, the makers of the Peppa Pig are Neville Astley and Mark Baker. They even served as the writers of the show along with Phillip Hall.

In the program, Daddy depicted as a cheerful person who likes to play with his daughter Peppa and son George. As of 2019, Peppa’s show was comprised of six series in total. In addition, the superhit project was released in 180 territories all over the world.
