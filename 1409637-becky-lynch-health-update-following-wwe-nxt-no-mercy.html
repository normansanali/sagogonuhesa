Becky Lynch Health Update Following WWE NXT No Mercy

August 02, 2024
blog

Becky Lynch prevailed in her match over Tiffany Stratton during WWE NXT No Mercy last night but it was not without suffering some battle scars.

Fightful Select reports that Lynch sustained a nasty laceration to her arm and had to have 11 stitches to close it up afterwards. The report further states that she is expected to be okay moving forward.

It appeared that the incident happened when she took a spinebuster from Stratton onto a trashcan.
