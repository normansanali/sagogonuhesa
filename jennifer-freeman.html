Jennifer Freeman is an American actress and spokesmodel. Her roles include the films You Got Served, Johnson Family Vacation, and True to the Game, and the series My Wife and Kids, Pump, and The Black Hamptons. She has also been credited as Jennifer N. Freeman.

Her mother, Teresa, is black. It appears that her father is white. Some sources state that her father is Jewish. It is not clear if that is accurate. Freeman is her mother’s maiden name. Jennifer has a daughter with her former husband, professional basketball player Earl Watson.
