Alexa Nisenson Facts

  • She has done competitive dancing.
  • In January 2015, Alexa Nisenson made her first TV show appearance by playing the role of Geraldine Chandler in an episode titled Quid Pro Quo of the horror drama series, Constantine.
  • She made her theatrical film debut by playing the role of Georgia Khatchadorian, Rafe’s 9-year-old smart younger sister, in the 2016 live-action/animated family comedy film, Middle School: The Worst Years of My Life, starring Griffin Gluck, Lauren Graham, Rob Riggle, Isabela Moner, Retta, Thomas Barbusca, Andy Daly, and Adam Pally.
  • From 2018 to 2019, she brought to life the character of Charlie, a young girl who is a spy for the Vultures until she defected to Morgan’s group, in the post-apocalyptic horror drama television series, Fear the Walking Dead, created by Robert Kirkman and Dave Erickson.
  • Alexa Nisenson co-starred on Fear the Walking Dead alongside the likes of Frank Dillane, Alycia Debnam-Carey, Elizabeth Rodriguez, Mercedes Mason, Colman Domingo, Daniel Sharman, Maggie Grace, Lennie James, and Jenna Elfman.
  • She is a huge fan of actress Blake Lively.
  • Her dancing credits include having been associated with the Abby Lee Dance Company.
  • As a voiceover artist, Alexa Nisenson has worked on projects like the short television series Summer Camp Island and the video game Farpoint.
  • She has described herself as kind, caring, loving, thoughtful, and loyal.
  • During her free time, she likes doing her hair and makeup.
