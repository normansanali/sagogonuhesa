Lil Baby Wiki/Biography

Born on 03 December 1994, Lil Baby’s age is 27 years as of 2021. He was born and raised in a well-settled Christian family from Atlanta, Georgia, United States. He is an American by nationality and has his belief in the Christianity religion.

He completed his early schooling at Booker T. Washington High School in Atlanta, Georgia, United States.
