Obituary for Aaron Strong –

The Milwaukee Police Department is looking into a fatal crash that occurred in the city’s downtown on a Wednesday morning.

At before 9 a.m., a 22-year-old male driving east in the 1100 block of West Wells Street allegedly ran a red light at North 10th Street and collided with a car driven by a 40-year-old Milwaukee man proceeding north on North 10th Street through a green light.

The Rev. Aaron Strong was the pastor of Grace Lutheran Church, located at 1209 North Broadway. Grace said on Facebook that Strong had served at the church for seven years and had two children and a wife back home.

The 22-year-old driver was allegedly going “at a high rate of speed,” according to the police.

They say that after the crash, both vehicles continued east and crashed into four parked cars.

Transported to the hospital, Strong ultimately passed away there.

A Thursday autopsy has been set, according to the Milwaukee County Medical Examiner’s Office.

Police have taken the 22-year-old driver into custody and said they will present the case to the Milwaukee County District Attorney’s Office in the coming days.
