Bachelorette Alum Kiptyn Locke Expecting First Child With Samm Murphy

July 11, 2024
blog

Another Bachelor Nation baby! Bachelorette and Bachelor Pad star Kiptyn Locke is expecting his first child with girlfriend Samm Murphy, he confirms exclusively to Us Weekly.

The Southern California-based businessman, 37, who was the runner-up for Jillian Harris' heart on season five of The Bachelorette in 2009, will welcome a baby boy with Murphy this summer. The couple met last year at a charity event in L.A.

"I've always looked forward to being a dad. It's something I've been so excited about," Locke tells Us. Of Murphy, 29 — who started her own medical distribution company and appeared on the reality show Opposite Worlds — he gushes, "Samm is an amazing woman — she's an athletic, active, intelligent woman running her own business. She'll be an amazing mom."

Locke previously romanced fellow Bachelor Nation star Tenley Molzahn, whom he met during the 2010 season of Bachelor Pad. They dated off and on until May 2014, and remain very close, both emotionally and geographically. (They live within a few blocks of each other in San Diego, Calif.!)
