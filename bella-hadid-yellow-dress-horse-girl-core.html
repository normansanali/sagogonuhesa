Here's Bella Hadid Playing With Ponies in a Dreamy Yellow Dress

August 29, 2024
blog

Bella Hadid has been living her best horse-girl life ever since she took a step back from modeling to focus on her health. But in her latest photo shoot, she melded her two worlds, posing in a mustard-yellow dress to promote her new beauty brand, Orebella, while prancing around a field with ponies and goats.

The dress is a breezy, slouchy maxi covered in a brown boho pattern—a design that feels both cottagecore in its romance and ranch-core because of the way Hadid chose to style it. Hadid wore the piece with a matching brown belt, short brown cowgirl boots with white details, and brown beaded bracelets. Her chestnut hair was down in natural waves, and she wore minimal makeup.

In the clip, she is seen smiling and running in the grass with the animals, in some moments sitting down to pet them.

“Home… @orebella link in bio to get on the list… launching may 2.2024
🌾🌬️🤍 shot by @yasminediba 🤍 music . miss @sza 🤍,” Hadid wrote in the post caption.

Orebella launches on May 2, and very little is known yet about the company. On February 27, the Kin Euphorics partner posted a short teaser clip on Instagram to announce the upcoming debut of her new project, showing a close-up clip of herself—skin glowing, hair flowing—with a shimmery floral filter over her face. Sade’s “Kiss of Life” played in the background. In the caption, Hadid wrote, “May 2. My passion. My baby. @orebella. Link in bio.”
