Jade Cargill Provides Update on Her In-Ring Status for WrestleMania 40

August 24, 2024
blog

Jade Cargill has burst on the WWE scene with a huge impact ever since she first stepped between their ropes. With fans excited to see more, Cargill recently dropped hints in regards to her status for their upcoming extravaganza, WrestleMania 40.

The former AEW TBS champion recently spoke with Denise Salcedo after the end of the WrestleMania kick-off press event. When asked about her status for the Show of Shows, Cargill played it smart and stated that fans would have to keep coming back to WWE television programming to find out about it.

“You’re going to have to see that. You’re just going to have to see. If I want it, I got it. I’m going to go out there and get it. Let’s just say that.”

Jade Cargill signed with WWE last summer after making a name for herself in their rival promotion, AEW. She reigned as the record-setting AEW TBS champion, a reign that lasted for an entire year.

Cargill made brief appearances on WWE television before finally making her in-ring debut. She was one of the surprise entrants in the Women’s Royal Rumble match and displayed an excellent showing in the melee.

With her very first appearance inside the squared circle being highly appreciated by fans, it would be interesting to see Jade Cargill perform in high-stakes match on the Grandest Stage of them all in April.
