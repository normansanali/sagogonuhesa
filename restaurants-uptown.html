e2 Emeril's Eatery

One of the country's most famous celebrity chefs has a new restaurant in uptown Charlotte.

Emeril Lagasse's restaurant, e2 Emeril's Eatery, opened in late January at the Levine Center for the Arts.

SLIDESHOW: e2 Emeril's Eatery in uptown

The e2 menu will feature an eclectic selection of appetizers, salads, seafood and steaks, as well as some of Emeril's New Orleans and Southern-style dishes, homemade desserts and modern American classics with a twist. The options include shrimp pizza with artichokes and herb pesto and New Bedford sea scallops with fennel, leek, mushrooms and mini potatoes. Click here to see the full menu.
