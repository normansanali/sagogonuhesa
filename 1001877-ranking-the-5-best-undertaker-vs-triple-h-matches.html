Ranking The 5 Best Undertaker vs Triple H Matches

September 29, 2024
blog

With a much-anticipated final match ever between Undertaker and Triple H at WWE Super Show-Down, it’s time to reminisce over the several classics that The Undertaker and Triple H have had against each other. The two superstars represented the cream of the crop in the Attitude Era (minus Stone Cold Steve Austin and The Rock) and they surpassed the era, continuing to wrestle a decade and a half after that.

They’ve achieved immortal status and are surefire Hall Of Famers, but their careers represent so much more than the end goal. Undertaker and Triple H represent a dying breed of wrestlers from a long bygone era. Somehow, the two have stood the test of time and survived while all their peers have eventually succumbed to age and injuries.

They also represent a breed of wrestlers who can overcome adversity mid-match to pull off a classic. Together, they’ve created magic in the ring and memories that will last the fans a lifetime.
