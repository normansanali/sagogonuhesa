Ed Donatell (born February 4, 1957 in Akron, Ohio) is a head coach for the San Francisco 49ers of the National Football League.

He most recently worked as the defensive coordinator for the University of Washington Huskies football team under Tyrone Willingham from January to December 2008. After a dismal 2008 season, he and his whole crew were fired. He formerly worked for the New York Jets as a special assistant. Donatell spent three seasons as the defensive coordinator for the Atlanta Falcons of the National Football League, working under head coach Jim Mora. Prior to that, he worked for the Green Bay Packers. He graduated from Stow High School in Stow, Ohio, then went on to Kent State University to obtain a master’s degree in administration. He is the father of three children and is married. After a two-year stint with the Denver Broncos from 2009 to 2010, he is now the defensive back coach for the San Francisco 49ers.
