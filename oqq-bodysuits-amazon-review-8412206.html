I Can Go Braless in These Ultra-Flattering Amazon Bodysuits

July 19, 2024
blog

There is something so incredibly satisfying about finding those rare diamonds in the rough on Amazon. While I’m all about designer athleisure and splurge-worthy beauty products, weeding out the junk to unveil affordable-but-effective beauty products, under-$10 basics and the most universally flattering $30 swimsuits and delivering them to my friends, family, and readers gives me the ultimate high. Each week I spend hours perusing TikTok, Instagram, and Amazon itself, ordering up a cartful of promising finds and testing them out so you don’t have to. One of my latest and greatest finds? A $12 ultra-flattering bodysuit that allows me to go bra-free. 

Oqq, an Amazon brand influencers keep going back to, makes some of the best athletic essentials I have found on the online marketplace. From booty-lifting workout leggings and shorts to crop tops and sports bras, I have been pleasantly surprised by many of the brand’s styles. I recently ordered up a haul of some of its most highly rated goodies, and it was this ribbed, spaghetti strap bodysuit that really rocked my world. You can snag a pack of three for $36, which comes out to just $12 apiece.

The bodysuits come in a whopping 20 color combination options to choose from. I selected the white, black, and coffee brown option, which all work with my fall and winter wardrobe, but I would have been happy with any of the others. The bodysuits are surprisingly high quality for the price — the 90 percent nylon and 10 percent spandex blend material offers the perfect balance of stretch and compression, with a little added snugness around the belly to perfectly flatter your curves. The thicker material allows me to go braless and smoothes things out all over while remaining comfortable enough for all day wear. The closure in the crotch area features plastic snaps, which are really easy to use and re-snap while wearing. Another bonus? They don’t ride up on the sides, like many similar styles. Available in sizes S to XL, I do suggest sizing down, as they tend to run slightly on the large side. 

I wear the Oqq bodysuits with jeans, sweats, skirts, and various other bottoms. They also look great underneath a cardigan or with a button down shirt. However, I have worn them solo and felt like I was wearing a top, not shapewear. Overall these bodysuits are incredibly flattering and comfortable, and deserve all the hype surrounding them. Once you slip into one you will understand why there are so many repeat customers on Amazon; one of the 1,400 five-star reviewers even said they’ve ordered five sets.

If you aren’t crazy about the spaghetti straps, Oqq makes a variety of similar styles, including a  strapless halter and square neck version with wide straps, and even sleeved options as well.
