Lee Jun-ho Relationship With Lee Se-young Explored!

And many viewers believed the couple was dating in real life as well as they posted multiple pictures of each other on their Instagram. However, they never dated. It has been 2 years since the show was released and they don’t appear to have been involved romantically.

Talking about their relationship, Se-young previously described Jun-ho as “the greatest partner.” She said,

We were the closest to each other, our communication was smooth, communicating with him acting-wise was also smooth since his personality is very affectionate and friendly, and it felt like we could discuss [many things] comfortably while filming.

The pair later won Best Couple at the 2021 MBC Drama Awards, adding recognition for their on-screen romance.
