How old was Albert Pujols when he made his debut?

July 16, 2024
blog

American baseball player, Albert Pujols was 21 years old when he made his debut. He played his first game on February 2, 2001.

Albert Pujols draft

When was Albert Pujols drafted? Who drafted Albert Pujols? Albert Pujols was drafted by St. Louis Cardinals in 2001.

Albert Pujols draft team

How many years did Albert Pujols play for the St. Louis Cardinals after he was drafted? Albert Pujols played for the St. Louis Cardinals for 10; from 2001 to 2011 after he was drafted. He later joined the Los Angeles Angels in 2012 until 2021, played for the Los Angeles Dodgers in 2021, and finally returned to St. Louis Cardinals in 2022.

Albert Pujols achievements and honors

How many achievements does Albert Pujols have in his career? What are some of Albert Pujols’ achievements? Albert Pujols has achieved quite a lot in his career. Some of these achievements include;

    • 11-time All-Star.
    • 2-time World Series Champion.
    • 3-time NL MVP.
    • NL Rookie of the Year.
    • Two-time Gold Glove Award.
  • Five-time Fielding Bible Award.
