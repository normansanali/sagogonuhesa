What Happens If You Don't Tip GrubHub? Technically, nothing happens if you don't tip on GrubHub, but it can have an effect on your delivery. Since GrubHub drivers can see if you tip, no tip means they will not want to accept your order. Your order can get bounced around waiting to get picked up.
