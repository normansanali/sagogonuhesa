Beyonce, Jay Z and Blue Ivy Take Bike Ride on Hawaiian Vacation

September 17, 2024
blog

Need some fresh lemonade to cool down? Beyoncé, Jay Z and the couple’s daughter, Blue Ivy, are staying busy during their Hawaiian vacation. The famous trio rode bikes together on a dirt road on Tuesday, June 21.

Queen Bey, 34, shared a photo of their outdoor excursion on her official website. In the pic, she’s riding a red bike, clad in a colorful striped dress and sandals. The rapper, 46, rides alongside, as Blue Ivy, 4, sits in a child seat behind him.

Bey and Jay have been looking more in love than ever during their getaway, weeks after Beyoncé dropped her album Lemonade, which mainly focuses on marital affairs and a broken marriage. On Monday, the singer posted photos of the lovebirds (married since 2008) kissing on the beach and playing with Blue. Their pride and joy built sand castles and later was dipped by her famous dad during what appeared to be a cute father-daughter dance number.

On the style front, Beyoncé paid homage to her smash visual album, Lemonade. Some of her outfits have included lemonade artwork and a lemonade crown. She even added a lemonade keychain on her straw bag.

The family getaway is a refreshing break for Beyoncé, who has been crossing the globe for her Formation World Tour. Up next, she is set to take the stage at Stadium of Light in Sunderland, England, on Tuesday, June 28.

Beyoncé isn’t the only one heading back to work. As Us Weekly exclusively revealed, Jay Z is working on new music.

“Jay is working on an album telling his side of things,” an insider close to the couple told Us last month. Bey previously contemplated divorce in 2014, but the twosome are now “better than ever.”
