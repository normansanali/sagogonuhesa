Release information

Promotional versions on a transparent purple lamé disk were later distributed to Ga-Olé Members via lottery. To become eligible, members had the duration of the fifth Ultra Legend set to accumulate at least 1,500 Member Points. Those meeting the requisite number of points were entered into 1 of a number of scheduled lotteries to win 1 of 500 copies of this disk. Ga-Olé Members could become eligible for subsequent lotteries for every additional 500 Member Points earned. Disks began to be distributed to winners from late July 2019. Unlike previous Ga-Olé Member prizes, these disks do not feature a unique serial number.
