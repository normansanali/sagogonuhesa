Khlo Kardashian Rocks Sheer Black Skirt with Belly Chain to Hulu Upfronts

September 18, 2024
blog

The Kardashians are gearing up for season three of their eponymous Hulu show (quick interruption to say it looks deranged/dramatic per usual), and Khloé Kardashian stepped out at the Hulu Upfronts to promote it! Her outfit? A crop top and sheer maxi skirt that she paired with a casual belly chain.

James Devaney//Getty Images

Khloé actually dropped a full set of photos from this look, which apparently she liked so much she made one of them her Insta profile pic!

In other Khloé news, she just hopped on Instagram so shut down "exhausting" speculation that she and Tristan are back together following Kim Kardashian showing up to support him at a game.

"Stop pushing this narrative," she wrote in an Instagram comment. "It's tiring. But I suppose You guys will continue the narrative you want regardless of what I say so what's the point. It's exhausting but I learned people will only understand to the level of their own perception. Most are stuck at believing the lies because it's the narrative they want to fuel. Have fun…."

She added that when it came to Kim supporting Tristan at the game, "Some things are just as simple as they seem. A family member supporting another family member ESPECIALLY during a difficult time in life. Example….. just how I support scott and will forever support him. He's my brother. It's just not on an nba stage. Sad new world, If there's no photos people think it really didn't happen but yes, I see Scott often. Some things really are just as they are."
