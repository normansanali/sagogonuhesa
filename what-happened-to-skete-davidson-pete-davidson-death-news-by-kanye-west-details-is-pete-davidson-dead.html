Peter Michael Davidson, also known as Pete Davidson, is an American actor and comedian. For eight seasons, from 2014 to 2022, he was a member of the cast of the NBC late-night sketch comedy program Saturday Night Live (SNL).

Early in the decade of the 2010s, Davidson made his acting debut in small-scale guest appearances on Brooklyn Nine-Nine, Friends of the People, Guy Code, and Wild ‘n Out.

In the 2019 comedy film Big Time Adolescence, Davidson both appeared in, and executive produced. He co-wrote and performed in the semi-autobiographical comedy-drama movie The King of Staten Island the following year.

He was shortlisted for a People’s Choice Award for Favorite Comedy Movie Star in 2020, among other honors.
