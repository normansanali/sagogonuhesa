WWE &amp; Peacock Pay Special Tribute To The Iron Sheik

July 14, 2024
blog

The world lost the Iron Sheik this week, and the pro wrestling world is in mourning. He was 81-years-old at the time of his passing, although his age is now in question. His legacy will certainly live on, no matter how old he was.

The pro wrestling world was in mourning after the Iron Sheik’s passing broke. Hulk Hogan, a fierce rival of Sheik’s, even paid tribute to him.

Peacock has updated their WWE listing to include a very special tribute section. They are now offering fans a chance to watch a ton of Iron Sheik matches and moments, all collected together in one section.

The Iron Sheik was a pop culture icon at the time of his passing. He had his share of struggles over the years, but he also left a lasting impression on the entire pro wrestling world.

Millions of fans are saddened over this great loss. Hopefully, his time on his earth etched out a legacy that will never go away. It seems that WWE and Peacock are doing what they can to make sure that fans do not forget.
