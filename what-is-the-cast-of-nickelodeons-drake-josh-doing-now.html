What Is The Cast Of Nickelodeon's 'Drake &amp; Josh' Doing Now?

September 15, 2024
blog

Drake & Josh was a Nickelodeon sitcom that premiered in 2004 starring Drake Bell and Josh Peck, who played stepbrothers that are completely different in every way, shape, and form. As teens that are already trying to figure out life individually, they are thrust together, forced to share a room, and forced to navigate their new family situation.

The cast of Drake & Josh was beloved by its audience. Almost every cast member continued acting once the show concluded in 2007, a few even continued working with Nickelodeon. From well-known faces like Yvette Nicole Brown to Miranda Cosgrove, some of the actors and actresses involved in this show have gone on to make numerous movies and TV series. Here is what the cast of Drake & Josh is doing now.

8 Allison Scagliotti (Mindy) Is Now A Musician And Activist

Allison Scagliotti played “Mindy” in the show, Josh’s enemy-turned-girlfriend. Since leaving the set, Scagliotti continued to act until 2018 when she appeared in a few shorts and one episode of Take One Thing Off. She has moved on to music and activism, sharing her talents on Instagram as she plays songs on the guitar and fights for movements such as “Say Gay,” supporting trans women, the Black Lives Matter movement, and the importance of voting.

7 Jerry Trainor (Crazy Steve) Is Involved With Nickelodeon Once Again

Jerry Trainor has continued to work in movies and television shows since his time as “Crazy Steve” on Drake & Josh. He went on to be cast as “Spencer,” the older brother in iCarly. The reunion show premiered last year, in which he has been heavily involved, reprising his brotherly role. Trainor has also been cast in the upcoming reprise of The Fairly Oddparents TV series that is currently in post-production.

6 Yvette Nicole Brown (Helen) Currently Has 3 Productions In The Works

In Drake & Josh, Yvette Nicole Brown makes frequent appearances as Josh’s work manager “Helen.” Brown has been in Hollywood for decades, racking up almost 150 titles on her resume. As of recently, she has already been in three television shows this year (Central Park, Alice’s Wonderland Bakery, and The Chicken Squad). She is also working on three more projects all currently in post-production, two of which are movies, and one will be a TV series.

5 Jonathan Goldstein (Walter Nichols) Has A New Movie Coming Out

Jonathan Goldstein was cast to play Josh’s dad and Drake’s stepdad, “Walter.” After his time with Nickelodeon, he went on to act in dozens of other movies and television shows. He has had five productions come out in 2020, one short that released earlier this year, and has recently spent his time working on a new movie. God Save the Queens is a new film that is currently in post-production.

4 Nancy Sullivan (Audrey Parker-Nichols) Has Likely Retired From The Big Screen

Nancy Sullivan was hired for the role of “Audrey Parker-Nichols,” Drake’s mom and Josh’s stepmom. She played this character until the end of the show in 2007 and has only been involved in ten projects since then, one of which was the Merry Christmas, Drake & Josh movie. Sullivan also added video game and video short actress to her resume, but has been inactive in Hollywood since 2017, also staying private with no social media presence.

3 Miranda Cosgrove (Megan Parker) Reprised Her Other Nickelodeon Role For 'iCarly'

Immediately following the series finale of Drake & Josh, Miranda Cosgrove went from playing “Megan Parker” to starring in Nickelodeon’s iCarly. She has also been involved in the Despicable Me franchise for over a decade, keeping her busy when she wasn’t acting in other projects. Most recently, she has been starring in the iCarly revival that premiered last year and is now in its second season, as well as voice acting for the upcoming Despicable Me 4.

2 Controversial Star Drake Bell (Drake Parker) Is Still Acting And Making Music

Drake Bell went from beloved Nick star to controversial celebrity, to husband and father. He got married in 2018, and just recently, he and his wife welcomed a son into the world. Besides adjusting to fatherhood, Bell has continued to pursue his passion for music by playing guitar and singing as well as acting. His upcoming project is a film entitled The Adventures of Bunny Bravo that is “completed,” but does not have a release date set.

1 Josh Peck (Josh Nichols) Is Balancing Family And Hollywood

Josh Peck has been a busy man over the last few years. Not only did he get married in 2017, but he has been learning to balance Hollywood and raising his son. He has been heavily involved in television shows recently, starring in Turner & Hooch last year and How I Met Your Father this year. Peck has also made a cameo on the revived iCarly series and has two films in the works.
